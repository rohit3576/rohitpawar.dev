About Me

I’m a Full Stack & AI Developer focused on building modern, scalable web and application-based solutions that solve real-world problems. I work with clients to turn ideas into fully functional digital products — from designing clean, user-friendly interfaces to developing robust backend systems and integrating intelligent AI features. My experience spans web development, application development, and machine learning integration, allowing me to create solutions that are not only visually appealing but also powerful and efficient under the hood.

I believe good software is more than just code — it’s about delivering value, improving user experience, and creating systems that are reliable, maintainable, and ready for real-world use. Whether it’s a business website, a custom application, or an AI-powered tool, I focus on building products that are fast, scalable, and tailored to the needs of users and clients. I’m always exploring new technologies and approaches to build smarter, more impactful solutions, and I’m open to freelance projects, collaborations, and opportunities where I can contribute to meaningful and innovative work.

🚀 Recent Focus: Currently diving deep into Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, exploring how to enhance LLMs with private knowledge bases to deliver more accurate, source-grounded AI responses. Also experimenting with Three.js to push the boundaries of immersive web experiences — because great technology should feel as good as it works.

💡 Beyond Code: When I'm not architecting full-stack applications or fine-tuning AI models, I contribute to open-source documentation, mentor aspiring developers on building their first portfolio, and explore the intersection of art and technology through creative coding.
